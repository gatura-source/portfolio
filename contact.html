Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

I offer a wide range of web development services including responsive website design, e-commerce development, web application development, website maintenance, and performance optimization.

What is your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and requirements. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects may take 1-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, I offer website maintenance services to ensure your site remains secure, up-to-date, and performs optimally. Maintenance packages can be tailored to your specific needs.

How do we get started with a project?

The process begins with an initial consultation to discuss your requirements and goals. After that, I'll provide a detailed proposal including scope, timeline, and cost. Once approved, we'll begin the design and development process.

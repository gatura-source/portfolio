About Me

Who I Am

I'm a Python developer and tutor with a strong interest in building practical, scalable solutions—especially in areas like web development, data science, and blockchain. My journey started during my computer science studies, and since then, I’ve been focused on creating tools that solve real problems, whether that’s a Flask-based web app, a FastAPI microservice, or a custom bot for automation.

Most of my work revolves around Python and frameworks like Flask, Django, and FastAPI. I also spend a lot of time working with data—whether through machine learning models or analytics pipelines—and I enjoy diving into cryptography and blockchain tech, especially Hyperledger Fabric and Bitcoin.

I contribute to open-source projects when I can, and I enjoy mentoring students in topics ranging from secure coding practices to algorithm design. Outside of tech, you’ll usually find me testing out a new idea, writing small tools and bots, or reading up on distributed systems and privacy-focused computing.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new design trends, contributing to open-source projects, or hiking in the great outdoors.
